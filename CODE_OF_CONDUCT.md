# Code of conduct

AXON-SRA discussions are technical working spaces.

Participants must:

- address the technical argument rather than the person;
- provide evidence for factual claims;
- keep criticism specific and actionable;
- disclose conflicts of interest;
- avoid harassment, threats, discrimination, and deliberate disruption;
- respect maintainer decisions on scope, architecture, and release readiness.

Disagreement is acceptable. Personal attacks and repeated bad-faith behavior are not.

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content and may restrict participation when necessary to protect the project.
